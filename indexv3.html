FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does Zoho implementation cost?
It depends on scope. A focused Zoho CRM implementation is an affordable starting point, while multi-app automation (CRM + Books + Inventory + a custom Zoho Creator application) is priced per module and integration. Every engagement starts with a free consultation and ends with a fixed, written quote — no surprises.
How long does implementation take?
A standard Zoho CRM setup typically takes 2–4 weeks. Custom Zoho Creator applications and full business automation rollouts usually run 6–12 weeks, depending on the number of workflows, data migration, and third-party API integrations.
Do you work internationally?
Yes — ZohoForge works with clients worldwide. All delivery is remote-friendly across time zones, with documentation, screen-recorded walkthroughs, and live training sessions included.
Can you migrate our existing systems?
Yes. We regularly migrate data and processes from spreadsheets, legacy ERPs, Salesforce, QuickBooks, and other tools into the Zoho ecosystem — with field mapping, validation checks, and parallel-run periods so nothing is lost.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Yes. After go-live you can choose an ongoing support plan covering monitoring, user training, enhancements, and priority fixes — or engage us ad hoc whenever your processes evolve.
Should we buy Zoho One or individual apps?
If you'll use three or more Zoho apps, Zoho One is almost always more economical and unlocks the full ecosystem. If you only need a CRM or accounting today, individual licences may be smarter. We advise on licensing as part of the free consultation.
